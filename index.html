Activating Waste Oil for Sustainable Aviation and Clean Mobility

OLEA is a circular energy platform transforming waste oil into measurable environmental impact and sustainable fuel pathways for aviation and urban mobility.

About OLEA

Vision

To activate waste oil as a strategic resource for the energy transition, particularly in hard-to-abate sectors such as aviation.

System

OLEA combines physical collection, digital traceability and incentive-based participation into a scalable upstream fuel infrastructure.

Why It Matters

Sustainable fuels fail without feedstock. OLEA addresses this challenge at the source: everyday waste oil.

How OLEA Works

Collect

Waste oil is safely collected instead of polluting water systems.

Verify & Track

Oil is verified, classified and digitally logged.

Allocate Impact

Users choose whether their contribution supports SAF, biodiesel or mobility.

Scale

Aggregated volumes form reliable clean fuel supply chains.

Environmental Impact

  • Prevents freshwater contamination caused by improper waste oil disposal
  • Supports Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) and biodiesel production
  • Reduces illegal reuse and infrastructure damage
