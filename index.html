About OLEA

OLEA is a circular value platform designed to close the gap between waste oil generation and sustainable energy demand. By combining traceable collection, incentive mechanisms and sector-based allocation, OLEA turns a widespread environmental problem into a scalable solution for aviation, energy and urban mobility.

Environmental Impact

1 liter of improperly disposed waste oil can contaminate up to 1,000,000 liters of freshwater.

OLEA prevents this irreversible damage by enabling controlled collection and conversion of waste oil into Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF), biodiesel and clean mobility services.

Why These Names?

OLEA
Derived from Latin, Olea means olive and oil. The olive branch has symbolized peace, continuity and shared prosperity throughout history.

OurOil
Represents “our oil” — a shared responsibility and a shared resource. Ouro also means gold in Portuguese, symbolizing the hidden value within waste.

Ouroboros
An ancient symbol representing infinity and circular continuity — a system where every output becomes a new input.
