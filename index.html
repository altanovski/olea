Activating Waste Oil for Sustainable Aviation and Clean Mobility

OLEA transforms waste oil into measurable environmental impact, Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) feedstock and clean urban mobility benefits.

About OLEA

Vision

Turning waste oil into a strategic resource for the energy transition.

System

A traceable, incentive-based collection and allocation platform.

Why It Matters

Sustainable fuels fail without feedstock. OLEA fixes the source.

Environmental Impact

1 liter of improperly disposed waste oil can contaminate up to 1,000,000 liters of freshwater.

OLEA prevents this irreversible environmental damage by enabling controlled collection and conversion of waste oil into SAF, biodiesel and public services.
