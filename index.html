Where Does Our Name Come From?

Olea means olive and oil in Latin — a symbol of peace and sustainability.


OurOil represents shared ownership and value creation. “Ouro” also means gold.


Ouroboros symbolizes the infinite cycle — the core of OLEA’s circular vision.
